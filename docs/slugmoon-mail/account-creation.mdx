# Account Configuration

Once you have created your account, you will need to configure it to suit your preferences. This includes setting up your profile, adjusting notification settings, and customizing your experience.

## Accessing the Settings Page

Follow the steps below to access the settings page:
1. Open the <span className="emoji-like-icon"><img src="/img/icon.png" alt="Slugmoon Emblem" /></span>  [<GradientText animated>Slugmoon Mail</GradientText> site](https://mail.slugmoon.com).
2. Log in with your newly created account credentials.
3. You will be redirected to your inbox.
4. Click on the **Wrench** icon located in the top right corner of the page.
5. This will open the main UI where you can access various settings and configurations.

# Send your first email

To send your first email using <span className="emoji-like-icon"><img src="/img/icon.png" alt="Slugmoon Emblem" /></span>  <GradientText animated>Slugmoon Mail</GradientText>, follow these steps:

1. Click on the **Compose** button located in the bottom right corner of the page.
2. In the **To** field, enter the recipient's email address.
3. Fill in the **Subject** and **Body** fields with your message content.
4. Click the **Send** button to deliver your email.
